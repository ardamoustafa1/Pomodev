### Description

**🍅 Pomodev - The Gamified Pomodoro Timer**

Pomodev transforms productivity into a game! Unlike other pomodoro timers, Pomodev makes focused work genuinely fun with:

**🎮 Gamification System**
- Earn XP for every completed pomodoro
- Level up and track your progress
- Compete with friends on the leaderboard
- Maintain daily streaks

**📊 Powerful Features**
- Customizable timer durations (25/50 min for deep work)
- Detailed daily/weekly/monthly statistics
- Task management with pomodoro tracking
- Beautiful dark mode interface

**💰 Completely Free**
- No premium tier, no ads, no limitations
- No registration required to start
- All features included forever

**👥 Perfect For**
- Students studying for exams
- Programmers doing deep work
- Professionals managing tasks
- Anyone who wants to be more productive

Try Pomodev now and make productivity fun!
